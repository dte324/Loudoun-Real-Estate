Begin by clicking your town name.

  • Ashburn

    A suburb 30 miles northwest of Washington, D.C. Ashburn is known for its master planned communities and stunning lakes. In recent years, Ashburn has experienced rapid residential and commercial development making it a desirable area to live and work.

  • Sterling

    The eastern-most locality within Loudoun County, Sterling is an established area with great access to local commuter routes. The Sterling area includes many large employers including many government contractors and other government agencies along with vast recreation areas.

  • Leesburg

    Leesburg, VA serves as a gateway between the more developed eastern-half of the county and the rolling hills and countryside of the western-half. Leesburg is full of history and small town charm despite more recently becoming a large commuter suburb of Washington, D.C.

Model Development

More than 500 recently sold listings were web-scraped from a popular real estate website to form the basis of the housing prediction model. This model specializes in single-family, townhouses, and condos in three localities situated in the eastern portion of Loudoun County, Virginia: Ashburn, Leesburg, and Sterling. This model was designed to provide users a fairly accurate estimate of their home's value.
