Price Baseline for Early Summer 2020.

  • Ashburn

    A suburb 30 miles northwest of Washington, D.C. Ashburn is known for its master planned communities and stunning lakes. In recent years, Ashburn has experienced rapid residential and commercial development making it a desirable area to live and work.

  • Sterling

    The eastern-most locality within Loudoun County, Sterling is an established area with great access to local commuter routes. The Sterling area includes many large employers including large government contractors and other government agencies along with vast recreation areas.

  • Leesburg

    Leesburg, VA serves as a gateway between the more developed eastern-half of the county and the rolling hills and countryside of the western-half. Despite more recently becoming a large commuter suburb of Washington, D.C., Leesburg remains full of history and small town charm.
