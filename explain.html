Why is my estimate off?

Model Development

More than 500 recently sold listings were web-scraped from a popular real estate website to form the basis of the housing prediction model. This model specializes in single-family, townhouses, and condos in three localities situated in the eastern portion of Loudoun County, Virginia: Ashburn, Leesburg, and Sterling. This model was designed to provide users a fairly accurate estimate of their home's value.
